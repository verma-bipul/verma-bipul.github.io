Data_at_umn

University of Minnesota Library also provides access to several datasets. For instance it subscribes to Statista. For a more comprehensive list visit the link below:

https://libguides.umn.edu/FindResourcesbyFormat/datasets

There is also a specific section related to economics here:

https://libguides.umn.edu/econdata

In addition, if one needs access to restricted-use datasets, one can contact the FSRDC at Minnesota. Here is their webpage:

https://mnrdc.umn.edu/

One will typically need a research proposal to gain access to restricted-datasets.

The government recently launched a website to smoothen the process of getting access to restricted data. Visit the following website:

https://www.researchdatagov.org/

One can read the eligibility requirement here: https://manager.researchdatagov.org/RDG_User_Guide.pdf
