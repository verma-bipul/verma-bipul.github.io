Reshaping_data

Fri, 30 Dec 2022

Pivot Longer

This involves reshaping data from wide to long format. This means that the length of new dataframe will increase vertically as more rows gets added to the dataframe.

Syntax:

pivot_longer(data, cols, names_to ="name", values_to = "values")

Pivot Wider

This involves reshaping data from long to wide format. This means horizontal expansion by adding new columns.
